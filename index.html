What We Do

Businesses and organizations are increasingly dependent on digital technologies, which are at risk of being disrupted by cyberattacks. ‘Cyber resilience’ is the ability for organizations like yours to prepare for, respond to, and recover from cyberattacks and security breaches. Cyber resilience is key to your operational resilience and business continuity.

Our simple, affordable service schedules and automates checks to identify common vulnerabilities in your public-facing IT services and assists in your Cyber Resilience. All checks are remote, without the need to install software and use the same kind of publicly available information cybercriminals use to find easy targets.

    Here’s how we help protect you:

    1. External Service Monitoring and Alerting: We’ll monitor and alert for external services that could put your data or systems at risk.

    2. Email Security Monitoring and Alerting: We’ll monitor and alert for important areas of cyber security within your email domain.

    3. Data Breach Monitoring and Alerting: We’ll search for your business data in known and new data breaches.

According to the "Cyber Security Breaches Survey 2023", 32% of businesses reported a cyberattack in the past 12 months. Don't let your business be the next cybercrime statistic.
